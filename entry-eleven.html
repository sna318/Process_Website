Thesis Weekly Update #11

April 15, 2024: Production work

Project Updates

This week, I continued refining the design of my cards, including the remaining 4 Swords cards and some revisions to cards in the Cups and Pentacles, which I originally designed prior to developing the hand gesture system. I’ve been in contact with Graphic Lab Printing NYC and plan to have 5 decks of the 40 Minor Arcana cards printed with them next week. They’re based in Long Island and anticipate shipping to take 1-2 days, though I can also stop by their shop in person if anything goes wrong.

In addition, I’ve begun revising the beginning section of my narrative booklet to include instructions for how to read tarot with my system and how to use the worksheet to guide a 3-card predictive reading.

I’ve focused my time these past few days working on the paper as well. I did most of my work on the primary research section, additional research from existing tarot reading books, and my design process. I plan to write the introduction and abstract after my other sections are completed. I’m also in pre production for the commercial I’m filming next week and am hoping to have a cut of it done by the first week of May.

The last thing I need to focus more on is the content and structure of the fundraiser page, which I want to be available in time for the showcase. I think I might do it on either Backerkit or Indiegogo, the latter which I have experience with from previous fundraisers. The next few weeks will be busy for sure, but I feel good about the timeline!
